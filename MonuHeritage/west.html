🏰 Gateway of India – The Iconic Mumbai Landmark

Symbolizing the historical significance and architectural grandeur of colonial India.

The Gateway of India was built to commemorate the landing of King George V and Queen Mary in 1911. It is an architectural marvel blending Hindu and Muslim styles and serves as a popular tourist attraction.
